The Deaf Assistant App

60 Second App Preview

BeAware's Deaf Assistant app is centered around inclusion and accessibility, and making deaf peoples’ lives easier by making everyday actions inclusive and diverse. Apart from being free, the app is open source and available here, so we would like to continue being the best with community input and support.

Download on iPhone The only free, no-ads & privacy-safe app   Download on Android The only free, no-ads & privacy-safe app

Features

ALERT

Turn your phone into an elite alerting tool with customizable alerts of short or prolonged sounds around you

SPEECH

Take advantage of your phone's powerful speech-to-text capability to transcribe, even while the app is in the background

TEXT

BeAware is the only app that can read text loud into your live phone calls, assisted by customizable preset phrases.
